IOT

2. IOT Devices

1. IOT devices

IOT devices are deivces that are on the ground level connecting data from its environment. These devices can be connected to the IOT platfrom via internet directly or indrectly through a hub.

2. IOT devices - telemetry

These devices are specifically placed to gather data about their environment. They dont perform any other action.
ex. temperature sensor, humidity sensor to monitor the wheather.

3. IOT devices - remotely controlled

These devices are used in the industry or at homes and performm their designated tasks. Apart from that, they gather telemetry information which helps in monitoring these devices and control them if situation arises.
ex. cars, industrial robots, washing machines.

4. IOT devices - Hub

Hub is a localised controller and a gateway to the IOT platform. A hub may connect to few or hundreds of IOT devices in the region via proprietary protocals and process the data locally before sending it to the IOT cloud system. Hub can be used in situations were immediate results should be drawn in order to make business critical decisions.
ex. autonomous cars, security systems
